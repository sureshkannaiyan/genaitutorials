Chapter 22: AI Applications and Real-World Projects

1. Introduction

Artificial Intelligence has transformed almost every industry by enabling automation, prediction, optimization, personalization, and intelligent decision-making.

Organizations use AI to improve efficiency, reduce costs, enhance customer experience, and create innovative products and services.

AI is no longer a future technology—it is actively transforming businesses today.

2. AI in Healthcare

Healthcare is one of the fastest-growing domains for AI adoption.

Applications

  • Disease Diagnosis
  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Drug Discovery
  • Patient Monitoring
  • Clinical Decision Support
  • Virtual Health Assistants

Example

AI models analyze X-rays, CT scans, and MRI images to identify diseases such as cancer and pneumonia.
